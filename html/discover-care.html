Proper Care

We have high standards of quality for our products. High-quality materials as well as a careful manufacturing process ensure the unique practicality and sturdiness of our products. You will enjoy your products for a long time if you carefully read and heed the instructions for care and storage that are in our user manuals.

You would like to care for your Petromax products properly? We recommend that you use the Care Conditioner for Cast and Wrought Iron, the Cleaning Polish for stainless steel and the matching Polishing Cloths.
